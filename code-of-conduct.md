# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Reporting

Report concerns to the maintainer at rdmgator11@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

For the full text of the Covenant, including community standards and enforcement guidelines, see the [official Contributor Covenant page](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
